Welcome to Fast Bézier Curve generator!

Use the mouse to place control points inside the canvas and press "Render" to draw the curve. You can reset by pressing "Clear".

"Curve edges" determine how many samples will be applied when rendering the curve.

You can re-adjust the control points with the mouse after they are placed. There are no limits to how many control points you can place, so go wild!

To resize the canvas, drag from the bottom-right corner.

Mouse Position: 0,0
